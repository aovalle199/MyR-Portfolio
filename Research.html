Current Research

By Andrea Ovalle

I added several papers that can further explain circadian rhythms and how their disruption might have several effects on health. I am also interested on how circadian rhytms might serve as markers for neurodegenerative diseases, so I added a really cool paper about that ! See Below

My favorite paper is Amyloid-β–Induced Changes in Molecular Clock Properties and Cellular bioenergetics Karen Schmitt,Amandine Grimm, and Anne Eckert (2017) I am interested in Biomarkers of AD, and this paper talks about how early circadian disruption might occur pre- mild cognitive impairment and thus be a marker associated with the disease.
